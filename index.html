    Blackjack Rules

    The goal of Blackjack is to beat the dealer’s hand without exceeding a total value of 21.
    - Number cards: Face value
    - Face cards (Jack, Queen, King): 10 points each
    - Ace: 1 or 11 points (whichever benefits the hand)

    Aces are flexible, and you can change their value based on what gives you the best total score. If you exceed 21, you “bust” and lose the round.
